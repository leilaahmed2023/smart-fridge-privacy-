Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 16, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how FloraGuide ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the FloraGuide mobile application. By using the app, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide and improve the app's features:

  • Account information: When you sign in with Google, we collect your name, email address, and profile photo via Firebase Authentication.
  • Plant scan images: Photos you take or upload are sent to Google's Gemini AI API for plant identification. Images are processed in real time and are not stored on our servers.
  • Scan history: Identified plant results are saved to your personal account in Firebase Firestore so you can review them later.
  • Usage data: Basic app usage information (e.g. number of scans used, ad credits) is stored in Firebase Firestore to manage your account's features.
  • Advertising data: The app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account.
  • To identify plants in photos you submit and return results to you.
  • To save your scan history so you can access it across sessions.
  • To manage scan credits earned by watching rewarded ads.
  • To display relevant advertisements through AdMob.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We share data only with the following trusted services required to operate the app:

  • Google Firebase – Authentication and cloud database (Firestore).
  • Google Gemini AI – Plant identification from images.
  • Google AdMob – In-app advertising.

All third-party services are governed by their own privacy policies and Google's data processing terms.

4. Data Retention

Your account data and scan history are retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the email below, and we will remove your information from Firebase within 30 days.

5. Children's Privacy

FloraGuide is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

6. Security

We use Firebase's industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including authentication rules and Firestore security policies. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting the app and contacting us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
